## Notes

- **Brand color** refers to the primary interactive color (`--nldesign-color-primary`) used for buttons, links, and accents. Some organizations have a separate brand/identity color for their logo (see [Token Architecture](tokens.md#brand-color-vs-interactive-color)).
- **Background color** is always `#FFFFFF` (white) as the base page background.
- Logos marked `--` are not yet available. The organization's website either does not provide an SVG version, or requires authentication to access it.
- The Leiden logo uses white (`#fff`) fills and is designed for dark backgrounds. It requires `--nldesign-color-logo-background` to be visible in the header.
- The Rijkshuisstijl logo (`rijkshuisstijl.svg`) is a copy of `img/nederland-logo.svg` placed in `img/logos/` for consistency with the theming sync system.
